Collections

Collections store groups of objects.

The most common collection is List<T>.

Example:

List<string> names = new List<string>();

Adding items:

names.Add("Alice");

Removing items:

names.Remove("Alice");

Collections can be searched, sorted, and iterated.

Useful collections include:

List<T>
Dictionary<TKey, TValue>
HashSet<T>
Queue<T>
Stack<T>

Choose the correct collection depending on the problem.
